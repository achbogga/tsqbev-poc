## Literature Read

High-ROI supervision ideas for the current repo:

1. Quality-aware ranking targets, not plain class logits.
   Source: Generalized Focal Loss, https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.04388
   Source: VarifocalNet, https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.13367
   Source: TOOD, https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2021/html/Feng_TOOD_Task-Aligned_One-Stage_Object_Detection_ICCV_2021_paper.html

2. Distill from stronger BEV teachers through BEV-space or response-space supervision, not only seed replacement.
   Source: DistillBEV, https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2023/papers/Wang_DistillBEV_Boosting_Multi-Camera_3D_Object_Detection_with_Cross-Modal_Knowledge_Distillation_ICCV_2023_paper.pdf
   Source: UniDistill, https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2023/papers/Zhou_UniDistill_A_Universal_Cross-Modality_Knowledge_Distillation_Framework_for_3D_Object_Detection_in_Birds-Eye_View_CVPR_2023_paper.pdf
   Source: BEVDistill, https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.09386

3. Keep the deployable ceiling anchored to a strong dense-BEV upstream.
   Source: BEVFusion official repo, https://github.com/mit-han-lab/bevfusion
   Source: NVIDIA DeepStream BEVFusion docs, https://docs.nvidia.com/metropolis/deepstream/7.1/text/DS_3D_MultiModal_Lidar_Camera_BEVFusion.html

## Decision

The next bounded loop should prioritize teacher-quality supervision over more query-budget or scheduler-only tweaks.

That means:
- keep the passed `teacher-anchor` regime as the baseline
- add quality-aware soft teacher class targets on teacher-seeded queries
- add class-aware teacher-region supervision around teacher boxes
- only then revisit box/logit distillation and dense-BEV control-arm work

## Next Bounded Agenda

1. `teacher_quality`
- add soft class-quality supervision using teacher labels and teacher scores on teacher-seeded queries
- add class-aware region supervision for nearby queries

2. `teacher_quality + box/logit KD`
- re-enable teacher distillation only after the quality-target path is running
- use the cached teacher boxes / labels / scores already present in the repo cache

3. `dense-BEV control arm`
- use the reproduced BEVFusion ceiling as the direction-setting control, not as the student target expectation for the tiny sparse runtime

## Stop Conditions

Stop spending more loop budget on sparse-query-only source-mix and small scheduler tweaks if:
- they do not beat `v16` quality after the new teacher-quality supervision lands
- or they keep the student at `100%` LiDAR-seeded source mix without raising mini `NDS`
